About Chuka Premier League

What is CPL?

Chuka Premier League is a two-division local football league. Every fixture, result, standing, and player profile on this site is pulled directly from the league's Google Sheet — updated by the admin team as matches happen.

Rules & Eligibility

Players must complete registration and payment before receiving a CPL number. A CPL number is required to play and can be verified on matchday via QR scan at /verify.

Contact

For questions about registration, transfers, or anything else, use the Enquiries page.
